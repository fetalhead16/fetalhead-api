Segmentation-driven fetal head analysis

Upload an ultrasound image and extract the biometric values instantly.

This research-demo app follows the paper workflow in your project folder: preprocess the scan, isolate the fetal head, fit an ellipse, and return HC, BPD, OFD, CI, and HA.

Frontend included Backend included Hostinger-ready packaging
Pipeline
  1. Upload ultrasound frame or DICOM
  2. Preprocess and detect head contour
  3. Fit ellipse and compute biometrics
  4. Show overlay and screening summary

If the file has no calibration metadata, the app still works but reports values in pixels instead of millimeters.
